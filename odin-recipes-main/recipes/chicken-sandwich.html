Buffalo Chicken Sandwich

Do you ever find yourself enjoying basic sandwiches, but find yourself wanting more? This Buffalo Chicken Sandwich will perk you right up while still containg basic ingredients you find at home. All you need is a little Buffalo sauce and a way to fry chicken; soon after your taste buds will serenaded in chicken-y goodness with just the right amount of kick. Perfect for one or to feed the whole family, these sandwiches are the perfect meal for on-the-go or a sit down meal at home

Ingredients

  • Buffalo or Any Hot Sauce of Your Choice
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Chicken Breast
  • Buns
  • Butter
  • Pickles or Any Toppings of Your Choice
  • Oil For Frying

Steps

  1. Pour the milk, hot sauce, and egg into the bowl and wisk well
  2. Pour oil into a frying pot and heat to 350F (176.7C) make sure the oil does not drop below this temperature
  3. Fry chicken for three to four minuets for each side then remove from pot
  4. Toast buns and butter
  5. Assemble sandwich and add pickles (or any toppings of your choice)
  6. Enjoy!
