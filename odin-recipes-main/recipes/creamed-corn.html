Creamed Corn

Creamed corn may be one of the most simplest dishes, but that doesn't detract from its taste. Wether it be for Thanksgivig, a basic Family get-to-gether, or just for yourself this recipe sure won't disappoint. Simple ingredients, simple to make, and simple to enjoy, it doesn't matter if you are a world class cheif or a new home cook you can't go wrong with creamed corn.

Ingredients

  • Canned Corn
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Parsley Flakes

Steps

  1. Open the can of corn and drain any and all liquids from the can and wash the corn if necessary
  2. Add the corn into the pot and add the butter and milk
  3. Cook at a medium to low heat
  4. Reduce the liquid untill there is very little left
  5. Turn off all heat and add the parsley flakes
  6. Serve and enjoy!
