Worcestershire Duck

Duck, although not the most popular protein is without a doubt my favorite. This recipe will show you a way to take the pesky waterfowl, and turn into into something delicious. The prep time may take while I can tell you for sure it will be worth it. Hopefully you and whoever else you make this for can gain a new appreciation for duck!

Ingredients

  • Duck Breast
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Creole Seasoning
  • Chili Powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Creole Seasoning
  2. Marinade the duck in the worcestershire for at least 12 hours
  3. After marinading remove the duck from the worcestershire and season with creole and chili powder and let set for a couple more hours
  4. Pan sear the duck at a medium heat
  5. Cook until done and set to cool
  6. While cooling set a small tab of butter on the duck this can not be done while the duck is still in the pan
  7. Plate and enjoy!
